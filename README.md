# phantom-prospector
Autonomous AI Lead Generation Agent

An AI-powered lead generation system that automatically discovers potential leads, extracts publicly available contact information, and generates personalized outreach messages.

🚀 Features
🔍 Lead Discovery
Searches websites, directories, and public sources to find potential prospects.
Filters leads based on industry, company size, location, and custom criteria.
📧 Email Extraction
Extracts publicly available business emails from websites and company pages.
Validates email formats before processing.
🤖 AI-Powered Outreach
Generates personalized cold emails using Large Language Models (LLMs).
Adapts messaging based on prospect details and business context.
📊 Lead Management
Stores and organizes leads in a structured database.
Tracks outreach status and generated messages.
⚡ Fully Automated Workflow
Find leads
Extract contact information
Generate personalized outreach
Export results
